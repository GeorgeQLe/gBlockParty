## Show notes

<Callout tone="info">
Weekly G is a personal vlog-style show. Less polished, more honest — think build-in-public for a dev creator.
</Callout>

### The purge

I deleted 40+ tutorial videos from the channel. Here's why:

- Serialized tutorials (Part 1, Part 2, ...) bled 80% of viewers between parts
- Monolithic builds consistently outperformed by 4–10x
- Old content was cannibalizing impressions from new uploads

### The new strategy

1. **Monolithic tutorials only** — one video, one complete project
2. **Weekly SOTA** — news recap, low production overhead, high surface area
3. **Weekly G** — behind-the-scenes, no script, no pressure

### What's next

Building gblockparty.com as the content hub. All tutorials, episodes, and clips in one place — no more relying solely on YouTube's algorithm.
